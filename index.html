Getting Started

×

In this game, you will be looking for 10 locations I have chosen as places I like to visit and places I want to visit. In order to find a location, you must be at a zoom level of 8 or higher. When you find a location, the map will reset and you can start looking for the next location. There are hints in the hint bar at the top of the screen if you get stuck. You can automatically win by double clicking the word "Game" in the title. You can click the × button or anywhere outside of this box to start the game. Good Luck!
